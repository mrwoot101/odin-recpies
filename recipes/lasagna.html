Lasagna!

Ingredients required for Lasagna:

    Meat Sauce
  • Beef Mince
  • Pastry sheets
  • Ricotta Mixture
  • Mozzarella Slices
  • Parmesan Cheese

How to cook Lasagna:

  1. Place a layer of pastry in the bottom of a large oven tray
  2. Place a fair amount of mince, cheeses and sauce then place
    another layer of pastry on top
  3. Continue doing so until satisifed
  4. place in oven at 180c for 60 minutes or until golden brown
  5. Remove to cool then serve
